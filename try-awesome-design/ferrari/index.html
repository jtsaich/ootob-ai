Ferrari Design Highlights

Chiaroscuro Rhythm

Deep black. The absence of everything. Quietude.

Clean white. Total clarity. The eye rests, then reads.

Black again. Each section is a page turn. Dramatic.

White returns. Contrast creates rhythm without decoration.

Red Sparseness

When red does appear, it carries the weight of the entire brand. One element out of six. Extreme sparseness is what gives Ferrari Red its power.

Editorial

The Art of Restraint

Ferrari's design language is defined not by what it includes, but by everything it deliberately leaves out. No shadows. No gradients. No ornament. Only surface, contrast, and precision.
